INTRODUCTION

With epic battles, striking animation and a gripping plot, Kimetsu no Yaiba: Demon Slayer has truly taken the world by storm. Often abbreviated as Kimetsu or KnY, the wildly popular franchise began in 2016 with a manga by Koyoharu Gotoge. Combining the genres of adventure, dark fantasy, and martial arts to tell the story of a boy named Tanjiro who becomes a powerful Demon Slayer, it went on to receive internationally acclaimed anime adaptations that firmly cemented its place in popular culture.

“But wait, I’ve never heard of this Demon Slayer thing, and what's a Tanjiro?” you might say. Or maybe you’ve just kind of heard of it and you want to find out more about must-know characters and keywords. Look no further than our beginner’s guide to the world of Kimetsu no Yaiba!

THE STORY

In Taisho-era Japan, Tanjiro Kamado is a kindhearted boy who makes a living selling charcoal. However, his peaceful life is shattered when a Demon slaughters his entire family. His little sister Nezuko is the only survivor, but she has been transformed into a Demon herself!
